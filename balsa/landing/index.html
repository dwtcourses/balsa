FAQ

  • What is Balsa?

    Balsa is a reliable, scalable knowledge management solution. It helps streamline information within the knowledge and collaborate among teams within an organization.

  • How secure is Balsa?

    We have a detailed security documentation that explains how we approach security within our team and as we develop Balsa. In short, we take backups daily for last 7 days, data is encrypted at rest and transit, we use a PCI Service Provider Level 1 payment processor and host your data on a Google Cloud Platform.

  • Can I use Balsa for free?

    Balsa is open source, hence you can use the Balsa Community Edition for free, by simply installing on your server. No questions asked, no strings attached. You can always upgrade to Team plan by getting in touch with us.

  • Do I have to self host Balsa?

    Balsa can be deployed on-premises with ease, but we also offer it in a fully managed and dedicated cloud setting. In either case you'll always get VIP support from our experienced team.
