What are Balsa Team Edition and Balsa Enterprise Edition?

Balsa Team Edition and Balsa Enterprise Edition are extensions to Balsa, which add more functionality and provide additional support options for customers.

How does licensing work?

Every organization running Balsa Team or Enterprise Edition on its own servers must have a license. There's no limit to the number of servers or environments used by that organization. The price is based on the number of users defined in production environment.

Do I have to self host Balsa?

While Balsa can be deployed on-premises with ease, we also offer it in a fully managed and dedicated cloud setting. In either case you'll always get great support from our experienced team.

Can I try Balsa before I buy?

You can try Balsa free for 30 days without any commitments in one of our data centers in United States, Europe or Singapore. Let us know and we'll setup your trial service.
